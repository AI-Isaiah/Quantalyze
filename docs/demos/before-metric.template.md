## What you're measuring

For each of 2 sample allocators from your existing Telegram network, you're going to:
1. Run your current manual matching workflow end-to-end
2. Time it with a stopwatch (real time, not "ideal time")
3. Screenshot every step (Telegram messages, spreadsheets, exchange data lookups, anything you actually open)
4. Note every micro-task: scrolling, copy-pasting, switching tabs, mental computation

The honesty test: would you be comfortable showing this exact recording to the cap-intro partner as evidence of "this is what manual matching looks like today"?
